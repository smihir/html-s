I joined Qualcomm as an Engineer in the Wireless Connectivity Team. Here we worked on developing the Firmware and the Linux Device Driver for Qualcomm Atheros' WCN series of Connectivity chips. My primary contributions to the team include design of Regulatory framework for the WCN drivers and my role as a maintainer of the DXE (A special DMA Engine) driver.

During my tenure in Qualcomm I was promoted to Senior Engineer level and have received several awards recognizing my contributions.
